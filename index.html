What is How-Ya-Reeling?


Welcome! We are a web service designed to assist you with deciding what movies to watch!

We use secure facial imaging technology to help you choose the perfect movie for any mood.

To use:

  1. Click on Take a Picture to take or upload a picture of your self
  2. Wait for How-Ya-Reeling? to predict your mood and recommend you movies to watch
  3. Check out our Features and Filters for more ways to customize your experience
  4. Select a movie you would like to watch
  5. Enjoy!
