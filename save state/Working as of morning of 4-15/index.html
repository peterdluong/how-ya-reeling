What is How-Ya-Reeling?

How-Ya-Reeling recomends movies based on your emotions!

To use:

  1. Click on Take a Picture to take a Picture
  2. Wait for How-Ya-Reeling? to predict your emotions recomend you movies to watch
  3. Select a movie you would like to watch
